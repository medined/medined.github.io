--- layout: post title: How Many Accumuo Loggers Are Needed Related To Slaves (tserver) Nodes? date: '2012-10-16T23:28:00.002-04:00' author: David Medinets categories: accumulo modified_time: '2012-10-16T23:31:39.623-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3207985.post-3844139724800877525 blogger_orig_url: http://affy.blogspot.com/2012/10/how-many-accumuo-loggers-are-needed.html year: 2012 theme: accumulo --- From the Accumulo User mailing list on Oct 10, 2012, John V says:
The scripts start loggers on all of the slave nodes. You can arbitrarily start/stop loggers, but we highly recommend doing 1:1 between them and tservers because the method for assigning tserver->logger does make assumptions depending on that 1:1 correlation.
