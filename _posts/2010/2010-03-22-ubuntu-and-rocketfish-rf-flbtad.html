--- layout: post title: Ubuntu and RocketFish RF-FLBTAD bluetooth dongle date: '2010-03-22T00:24:00.001-04:00' author: David Medinets modified_time: '2010-03-22T01:00:30.290-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3207985.post-676581916937532365 blogger_orig_url: http://affy.blogspot.com/2010/03/ubuntu-and-rocketfish-rf-flbtad.html year: 2010 --- I bought the RocketFish RF-FLBTAD bluetooth dongle today. When it was plugged into the front USB ports of my computer, the dongle would not be recognized as an HCI device. However, when I plugged it into one of the USB ports in the back of my computer, it registered just fine. Lots of good messages in /var/log/syslog. Update: The dongle disappeared when I rebooted. However, it came back when I manually ran: sudo /etc/init.d/bluetooth restart
