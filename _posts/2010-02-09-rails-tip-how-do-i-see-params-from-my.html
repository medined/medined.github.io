--- layout: post title: Rails Tip - How Do I See Params From My Forms? date: '2010-02-09T09:07:00.002-05:00' author: David Medinets tags: modified_time: '2010-02-09T09:13:06.162-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3207985.post-3533489449485734501 blogger_orig_url: http://affy.blogspot.com/2010/02/rails-tip-how-do-i-see-params-from-my.html --- I like to document what my forms are sending to my action methods. So most of the time, I create a method in my controller like this: 
def to_export
  render :text => params.inspect
end
Then I use copy and paste to put the result into my code as a comment.
