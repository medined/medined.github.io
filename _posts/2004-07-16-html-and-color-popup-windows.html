From their site: The HTML Popup is a tall skinny on-screen quick reference showing all HTML tags and attributes in use or universal. It includes color-coded indications of support by Netscape®, Microsoft® Internet Explorer and W3C®, plus style sheet alternatives, and bug and clash icons with details online. The Color Popups show all 216 web-safe colors in a wheel.
